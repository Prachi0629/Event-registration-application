IoT WORKSHOP

IOT is a system of interrelated things, computing devices, mechanical and digital machines, objects, animals, or people that are provided with unique identifiers.Basically IoT is the network of physical devices.End-to-end, remote monitoring and management of assets/resources.

Learning IoT and its Fundamentals :-

Privacy Concerns

There is the collection and use of personal data by IoT devices & the risk of unauthorized access comes in Privacy concers. Using various authentication and authorization techniques these problems can be overcomed.

Wireless Connectivity

Allows devices to connect and share data without being tethered to a specific location or device. Various IoT gateways and communication devices are included here.Ex.] Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, zigbee, cellular networks.

M2M Communication Protocols

In M2M there is exchange of data without human intervention. Protocols are the set of rules to establish connection among any system/devices
world-wide.Protocols like MQTT, CoAP, XMPP, TCP/IP, UDP are used commonly.

Cloud Integration

Connects IoT devices at cloud server where data storage, analysis, processing is done. IoT devices uses sensors and the internet for gathering the information.
Definiteky stored at clouds. Cloud integration provides scalability, accessibilty, real-time updates, etc.
